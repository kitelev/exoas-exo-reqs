# req(exo): the property editor hardens the reify/de-reify toggle ‚Äî strong-confirm de-reify, object-side read-only, destination-AS picker, desktop‚Üîmobile parity

## Job Story

When I de-reify a relation (pull it back from a private statement asset into my open asset's
inline frontmatter), I want a deliberate, hard-to-fumble confirmation that spells out what is
about to happen ‚Äî the statement asset will be deleted, and the relation will become inline and
therefore travel publicly when I share the asset ‚Äî so I never expose a private relation by an
accidental click. When I look at a reified relation from the OTHER end (the asset it points AT),
I want to see that it is reified but NOT be able to toggle it from there, because toggling would
silently edit someone else's asset. And when I reify a relation, I want to choose which private
AssetSpace the statement goes into ‚Äî that choice IS the privacy decision. All of this must work
the same on my phone as on my desktop.
