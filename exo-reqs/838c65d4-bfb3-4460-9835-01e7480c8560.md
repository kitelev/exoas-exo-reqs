# req(exo): the Relations block shows a clickable link-icon to the backing `exo__Statement` for reified relations (replacing the factual `reified · <AS>` text marker)

> **Supersedes `a4c8a9a0`** — that requirement specified a FACTUAL grey text marker
> `reified · <AS>` (RFC 93a0b2ee §C2, Phase 2, merged PR #3762). Andrey's interview
> 2026-07-12 evolved that marker into a clickable icon-link that opens the backing
> statement asset, with the AssetSpace moved to the icon tooltip. Because the literal
> text-marker Gherkin of `a4c8a9a0` no longer holds, `a4c8a9a0` is flipped to
> **Deprecated** (a conscious withdrawal — the SDD invariant forbids "active but broken");
> this requirement carries the concern forward.

## Job Story

When I open an asset whose relations include one that is reified (backed by an
`exo__Statement` asset), I want a small clickable link-icon next to the related asset
that opens the statement asset directly, with the AssetSpace it lives in shown on hover —
so I can jump to the reifying statement in one click, without a bulky visible text marker
and without the UI ever making an unverifiable privacy promise.

## Statement (Gherkin)

```gherkin
Given the Relations renderer component receives a list of relations
  And one relation carries provenance "reified" WITH a statementPath (the backing
      exo__Statement asset) and the backing statement's AssetSpace
  And another relation carries provenance "inline" (or no provenance — legacy)
When the Relations block renders
Then the reified relation shows a CLICKABLE icon-link in the name cell rendered with the
     native lucide "link" glyph via Obsidian setIcon (no emoji, no new column, no visible
     text marker)
  And clicking that icon opens the backing exo__Statement asset via the same onAssetClick
     handler used for asset links, passing the statementPath (Cmd/Ctrl-click → new pane)
  And the icon carries a hover/aria tooltip that names the AssetSpace the statement lives
     in AND hints at opening the statement (e.g. "Открыть связь · reified · <AS>")
  And a reified relation whose statementPath is NOT resolvable shows NO icon
     (there is nothing to open)
  And the inline relation shows NO icon
  And a relation explicitly tagged inline shows NO icon even if it carries an AssetSpace
     (the icon keys strictly off provenance === "reified" AND a present statementPath)
  And the icon and its tooltip contain NO privacy claim (no "🔒", no "не уедет" / "не уйдёт")
  And whenever at least one reified relation WITH a statementPath is present a PERSISTENT
     explanation legend is rendered below the table (visible without hover — readable on
     mobile) that describes the icon-link (click to open the reified statement) and states
     only WHERE the statement lives
  And ordinary (non-reified) rows are unchanged (no icon, no legend)
```
