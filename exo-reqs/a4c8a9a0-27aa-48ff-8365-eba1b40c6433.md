# req(exo): the Relations renderer shows a factual `reified · <AS>` marker for reified relations, no marker for inline, with a persistent explanation and zero privacy claims

## Job Story

When I open an asset whose relations include one backed by an `exo__Statement` asset
(a reified relation), I want the Relations block to show a small factual marker
`reified · <AS>` telling me WHERE that statement lives (in which AssetSpace) — and to
show no marker on ordinary inline relations — so that I can see at a glance which
relations are reified and where their statement asset lives, without the UI ever making
an unverifiable privacy promise it cannot keep.

## Statement (Gherkin)

```gherkin
Given the Relations renderer component receives a list of relations
  And one relation carries provenance "reified" with the backing statement's AssetSpace
  And another relation carries provenance "inline" (or no provenance — legacy)
When the Relations block renders
Then the reified relation shows an inline marker reading "reified · <AS>"
     where <AS> is the AssetSpace the backing statement asset lives in
  And a reified relation whose AssetSpace is not derivable shows a bare "reified"
  And the inline relation shows NO marker
  And a relation explicitly tagged inline shows NO marker even if it carries an AssetSpace
     (the marker keys strictly off provenance)
  And the marker text contains NO privacy claim (no "🔒", no "не уедет" / "не уйдёт")
  And whenever at least one reified relation is present a PERSISTENT explanation legend
     is rendered below the table (visible without hover — readable on mobile)
  And that legend likewise contains NO privacy claim (honest-marker: 0 unverifiable
     statements — it states only WHERE the statement lives)
```
