# req(exo): advancing onboarding via a result-modal next-step CTA marks the matching panel step done (idempotent; no-op if the panel is closed)

## Job Story

When I advance the first-run onboarding sequence by clicking a **result-modal next-step CTA** (the «Engine ready» panel's "Add the AssetSpace registry") instead of the onboarding panel's own Step 3 button, I want the panel's matching step to be ticked **✓ Done** — so the checklist reflects what I actually did and does not desync, while a repeat click keeps a single marker and a closed panel is never touched.

## Statement (Gherkin)

```gherkin
Given the first-run onboarding panel is open with Step 3 ("Add the AssetSpace registry") un-ticked
  And the durable bootstrap-result modal is stacked on top after a successful bootstrap
When I click the result modal's "Add the AssetSpace registry" next-step CTA
Then the onboarding panel's Step 3 is marked "✓ Done"
  And no other panel step is ticked
  And clicking the CTA again keeps a single "✓ Done" marker on Step 3 (idempotent)
  And if the panel has already closed, the CTA marks nothing and does not throw
```
